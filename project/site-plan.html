Call Your Neighbour for Service

Website Planning Document

Site Name

Call Your Neighbour for Service

This name clearly communicates the purpose of the platform - connecting neighbors who need services with those who can provide them. The spelling "Neighbour" adds a friendly, community-oriented feel while making it distinct for branding purposes.

Optional domain availability: callyourneighbour.org or neigbourservice.net

Site Purpose

The Call Your Neighbour for Service website will:

  • Provide a digital platform for local community members to offer services
  • Enable neighbors to discover and request services from people in their area
  • Facilitate direct communication between service providers and seekers
  • Showcase my personal services including web development, printing, and fashion design
  • Foster stronger community connections through local economic support

Scenarios

Example questions from potential site visitors:

  1. "Who in my neighborhood offers home repair services, and how can I contact them?"
  2. "I want to offer tutoring services to local students - how do I create a profile on this platform?"
  3. "Where can I see examples of the website creator's fashion design work?"
  4. "How do I know if a service provider is trustworthy?"

Color Schema

The color palette is designed to evoke trust, community, and approachability:

#4a6fa5
(Primary)
#63a375
(Secondary)
#f5f5f5
(Background)
#333
(Text)

Color Applications:

  • #4a6fa5 (Primary Blue): Headers, primary buttons, navigation
  • #63a375 (Secondary Green): Accent elements, secondary buttons, highlights
  • #f5f5f5 (Light Gray): Background color for content sections
  • #333 (Dark Gray): Primary text color

Typography

The typography system uses clean, readable fonts with a friendly tone:

Primary Font: 'Segoe UI', Tahoma, Geneva, Verdana, sans-serif

This is the main body font used for paragraphs and general content.

Heading Font: 'Trebuchet MS', 'Lucida Sans Unicode', 'Lucida Grande', 'Lucida Sans', Arial, sans-serif

Used for all headings (H1-H6) to create clear visual hierarchy.
